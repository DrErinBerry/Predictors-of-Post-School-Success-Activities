â­ Key Principles for Career Development Progression

  • Sequential Development: Each predictor builds on the previous one, creating a comprehensive pathway from awareness to employment.
  • The SPIN Profile Evolves: Students continuously develop their understanding of their Strengths, Preferences, Interests, and Needs through experiences.
  • Paid Employment is Critical: Research shows paid employment of ANY kind during high school is the strongest predictor of post-school success.
  • Age-Appropriate Activities: Activities should match developmental levels while still exposing students to authentic workplace experiences.
  • Family Involvement: Families should be engaged at every stage, discussing careers and supporting exploration.
  • Oklahoma Resources: Utilize CareerTech Centers, BEST STEP, DRS Pre-ETS, and other state programs to support career development.
  • Individualization: Not all students will follow the same timelineâ€”some may accelerate, others need more time at certain stages.
  • Integration is Key: Career development should be embedded across all classes and school activities, not isolated to one program.
