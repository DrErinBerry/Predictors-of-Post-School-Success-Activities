üìù Additional Notes

üí° Tips for Success

  • Start with the SPIN profile - Everything should align with student strengths, preferences, interests, and needs
  • Build progressively - Move from exploration (job shadowing) to experience (internships) to employment (paid work)
  • Focus on paid employment - It's the strongest predictor! Prioritize connecting students to competitive pay opportunities
  • Plan for fading supports - Goal is maximum independence, not permanent support
  • Document everything - Use this plan in IEP meetings, progress reports, and transition planning
  • Leverage partnerships - Oklahoma has excellent resources through CareerTech, BEST STEP, and DRS Pre-ETS
